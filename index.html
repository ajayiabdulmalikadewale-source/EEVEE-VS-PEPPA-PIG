Peppa gameplan

Chaos-first pressure

EARLY ROUND EDGE
  • Muddy Momentum: open with something loud and goofy; the opponent wastes turns reacting instead of planning.
  • Family Assist: support characters jump in as cameos, adding mini buffs like “louder cheering” or “snack break heal”.
  • Giggle Guard: refuses to tilt; even a “loss” turns into a story Peppa retells as a victory later.
Eevee gameplan

Download, then punish

LATE ROUND EDGE
  • Pattern Scan: first exchanges are “data only”—light hits, lots of dodging, reading exactly how Peppa swings the chaos.
  • Form Shift: adjusts “type” mentally: faster, tankier, or trickier depending on what just happened.
  • Safe Closer: when ahead on points, plays boring but perfect; the fun part was getting the lead, now it’s about not dropping it.
