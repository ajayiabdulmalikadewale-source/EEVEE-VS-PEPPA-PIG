About The Project

Why This Exists

This project was born out of a simple question: In a battle between absolute chaos and infinite adaptability, who wins? Peppa Pig represents the unstoppable force of toddler energy, while Eevee represents the potential to become anything. It is the ultimate crossover event for the modern era.
